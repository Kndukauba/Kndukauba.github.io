Verified Support Centers

National Center for Gaming Disorders

UK-based clinic providing clinical treatment and family therapy for individuals affected by gaming disorder.

Online Gamers Anonymous

A 12-step recovery program modeled after AA for those battling compulsive gaming behavior.

SMART Recovery

Offers science-based cognitive tools and group support for all forms of addiction, including gaming.

Game Quitters

Global support platform providing videos, forums, and coaching for quitting or moderating gaming.
