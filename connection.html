Connections

Join a supportive community where you can connect with others who are working to overcome gaming addiction. Participate in mentorship programs, discussion forums, and group challenges to build accountability and find encouragement.

Start Building Meaningful Connections

Whether you're working to reduce your gaming time or supporting someone who is, online communities offer encouragement, shared strategies, and valuable insight from people who understand the journey.

Recommended Support Communities

Game Quitters Community

This global support community offers structured coaching, a peer discussion forum, and helpful video content for anyone dealing with video game addiction.

Reddit - r/StopGaming

This active subreddit allows users to share experiences, discuss relapses and progress, and find motivation through a large anonymous support network.
