What Is Gaming Addiction?

Video game addiction is a behavioral condition where individuals compulsively play games to the extent that it disrupts their daily life, responsibilities, and relationships. Signs include excessive focus on gaming, irritability when not playing, neglect of hygiene or obligations, and loss of interest in other activities.
