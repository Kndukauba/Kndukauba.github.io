Practice Exercises

Scheduled Gaming & Time Blocking

What to do: Set time limits using alarms or apps. Follow a consistent routine to control playtime.

30-Day Digital Detox

What to do: Take a full month off gaming. Replace screen time with hobbies, fitness, or social events.

Trigger Mapping

What to do: Write down your gaming triggers and three non-gaming alternatives for each.

Purpose Rebuilding

What to do: Reflect on your goals, values, and how gaming can be redirected toward a purposeful life.
